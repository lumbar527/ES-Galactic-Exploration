Tri-War:

A war between three alien factions of the same species.

Axehead
 - Ships: Axehead Industries
 - Weapons: Archery Weapons (missiles)
 - Systems: Diamond Systems (shields)

Knights
 - Ships: Javelin Labs
 - Weapons: Hammer Industries (close range, inaccurate)
 - Systems: Might Manufacturing (power)

Storyline:

Random missions for one faction. There are random events of systems being conquered, and it ends when one faction has all the systems.
